Praia do zavial- Portugal Praia do zavial- Portugal




Namaste and thank you for sharing time with me here. This platform has a special place in my heart.

Emma Mildon said it best, “I am an expert in nothing and an explorer of everything.”

Some of the information on here may deeply resonate with you, some of it may seem far out there.

I have always thought that the spiritual journey is not about learning anything new,

but dissolving any illusions and remembering who we always were.


A little about myself- I grew up in a small, very friendly and safe town.

However, I moved to a big city in a new country, this challenged me in many ways

I found myself almost giving up and going back home to where it felt safe

It wasn't until I wondered into a yoga studio on a dull afternoon that I regained

my motivation and inspiration to keep persuing my dreams
